SaveZar Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SaveZar ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our taxi management application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and account credentials when you register.
  • Location Data: Real-time GPS location data from taxis for fleet management purposes.
  • Driver Information: Driver profiles, ratings, and performance metrics.
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, feature interactions, and session information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing real-time taxi location tracking and fleet management services
  • Managing driver profiles and performance monitoring
  • Generating statistical reports and analytics
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Communicating with you about your account and services

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our application
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Business partners with your consent

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request data portability

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
