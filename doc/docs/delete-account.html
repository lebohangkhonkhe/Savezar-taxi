Request Account Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data. Once we receive your request, we will:

  • Verify your identity using the email address provided
  • Delete your account and personal information within 30 days
  • Send you a confirmation email once the deletion is complete

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your user account and login credentials
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Driver profile data (if applicable)
  • Location history and tracking data
  • Usage statistics and preferences

Data That May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for legal compliance or legitimate business purposes:

  • Transaction records (as required by law)
  • Anonymized statistical data
  • Data necessary for legal claims or disputes
